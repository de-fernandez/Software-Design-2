About This Project

Learn about our vision, mission, and the people behind the research.

Our Vision

To make it easy for farmers and vendors to check tomato quality using smart tools, so they can avoid waste and sell better products.

Our Mission

Our goal is to create a simple and reliable system that can detect tomato defects using AI, so anyone can use modern technology even without technical knowledge.
